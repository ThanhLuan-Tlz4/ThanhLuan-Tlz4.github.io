# Reflection Report “Enterprise Cloud Architectures and Industry Application featuring Cloud Kinetics & Renova Cloud”

### Purpose of the Event

* Share experiences from seniors who have been working in the industry for many years
* Provide orientation and ways to develop in the current AI environment
* Use AI as a supporting tool instead of becoming dependent on it
* Understand how to behave in the workplace and how to be open in a professional environment

### List of Speakers

* **Nguyễn Gia Hùng** Job Market & Career Trends
* **Bành Cẩm Vỉnh** Sharing work experience as a Data Engineer
* **Như Trần** "How to have Vietnamese Steve Jobs"
* **Khang Nguyễn** AI Fresher Skill Mindset

### Highlighted Content

#### Job Market & Career Trends

* Introduction to the Cloud market in Vietnam
* History of AWS Vietnam entering the Vietnamese market from 2015 to present
* Current customer requirements for programmers
* Partners and job opportunities in the AWS community.
* Sharing the average salary of programming positions in the market.
* The shrinking market and the increasing requirements for programmer positions
* **Treat your boss and customers like your lover**

#### Sharing work experience as a Data Engineer

* The difference between the enterprise environment and school.
* Sharing how to divide tasks into a DNA of DATA PLATFORM
* Sharing his experience after working for 5 years at multiple companies
* Sharing his experience on how to use AI in learning and work, and the tasks that require humans and cannot be replaced by AI
* **Main idea** essential foundational knowledge, tools change continuously, every choice has a cost, community is a skill, AI cannot replace us.

#### "How to have Vietnamese Steve Jobs"

* Her sharing was psychological and gentle
* Sharing about fears when entering the workplace
* Improving communication in the workplace and relationships between colleagues
* How to seize opportunities that come to you
* **It is not that we are looking for work, but that we are looking for ourselves**

#### AI Fresher Skill Mindset

* Sharing the journey from studying to working
* Mindset in learning and working
* The difference between school and the real working environment
