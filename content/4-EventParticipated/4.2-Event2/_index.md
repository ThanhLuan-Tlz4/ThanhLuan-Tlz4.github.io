# Reflection Report “AWS MEETING DAY”

### Purpose of the Event

* Share best practices in modern application design
* Introduce DDD methodology and event-driven architecture
* Provide guidance on choosing suitable compute services
* Introduce AI tools that support the development lifecycle

### List of Speakers

* Sharing from the Director of the AWS AI Department about opportunities in the current information technology field
* Using infrastructure in Godot games
* Learning about Docker and virtual machines
* Sharing from an IT Help Desk professional with 5 years of experience and the roadmap to becoming a DevOps Engineer

### Highlighted Content

#### Sharing from the Director of the AWS AI Department about opportunities in the current information technology field

* He shared about the power of AI and job opportunities in the current field
* If he could go back to his student days, what would he do in the current information technology era
*

#### Using infrastructure in Godot games

* How to apply AWS infrastructure to game programming
* Research different ways to connect from the game engine and choose the most suitable approach
* Product DEMO: a simple rock-paper-scissors game built in the Godot game engine

#### Learning about Docker and virtual machines

* Learn the basic theory of Docker and the power of Docker compared to other applications
* Compare Docker with traditional virtual machine infrastructure
* Deploy several commonly used Docker commands
* Demo the execution speed of Docker in a computer environment

#### Sharing from an IT Help Desk professional with 5 years of experience and the roadmap to becoming a DevOps Engineer

* Learned the roadmap from IT Help Desk to System Admin and DevOps
* Shared system-related experiences during the working process
* Common difficulties in the job and problems encountered during work
