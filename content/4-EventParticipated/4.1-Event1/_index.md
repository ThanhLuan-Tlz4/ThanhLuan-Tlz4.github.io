# Reflection Report “AWS MEETING DAY”

### Purpose of the Event

* Share best practices in modern application design
* Introduce DDD methodology and event-driven architecture
* Provide guidance on choosing suitable compute services
* Introduce AI tools that support the development lifecycle

### List of Speakers

* **Hoàng Văn Khoa** Effective use of AWS and AWS-Cloud-Quest-Cloud-Practitioner.
* **Huỳnh An Khương** Introduction to the Hackathon program
* Fear when first learning Cloud and lack of confidence
* Introduction to the **Tử Vi Đại Việt** product and its development process on AWS
* Teamwork experience in real-world projects.
* Methods for overcoming procrastination and maintaining learning motivation.

### Highlighted Content

#### Effective use of AWS and AWS-Cloud-Quest-Cloud-Practitioner.

* Concern about not knowing how to use credits properly
* Risks of not cleaning up services, leading to credit shortages and real charges
* Introduction to several ways to practice AWS without using services directly, helping save credits
* Introduction to the **AWS-Cloud-Quest-Cloud-Practitioner** game, a platform for learning and playing with AWS services without spending credits

#### Introduction to the Hackathon program

* Introduction to your group’s programming competition
* Sharing how your team won the prize
* The joy and experience that hackathons bring
* Inspiring everyone through competitions such as hackathons

#### Fear when first learning Cloud and lack of confidence

* Fear of not being able to fit in and learning in the wrong direction when joining the AWS program
* Benefits of having a well-structured AWS learning plan
* Some tips for learning AWS effectively

#### Introduction to the **Tử Vi Đại Việt** product and its development process on AWS

* Introduction to a Vietnamese fortune-telling application built on the AWS Cloud platform by former Swinburne students.
* The application can predict near-future types of destiny through name and date of birth.
* Viewed the structure from AWS to the application infrastructure model.
* Had the opportunity to experience the fortune-telling application directly

#### Teamwork experience in real-world projects.

* A student from FPT University shared about the process of working on the Freeplan project.
* The process of overcoming communication hesitation and the student’s experience.

#### Methods for overcoming procrastination and maintaining learning motivation.

* Understood that the reason behind procrastination is fear
* The fears that lead to procrastination at work
* The harmful effects of procrastination in learning and daily life
* Some methods to overcome personal procrastination
